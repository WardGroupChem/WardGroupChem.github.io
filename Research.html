My Research


Using synthetic chemistry and molecular design to create bespoke molecules that perform a useful function.

In my lecturer position at Lancaster University I will be drawing upon all my expertise to develop fluorescent materials for security inks with unique security features and to develop highly robust pi-conjugated photocatalysts that are photobleach resistant for incorporation in flow chemistry systems.
